Use standard (XXL)DDrace entities


Special entities : 


  -Kill : Finish line (Round finished)

  -Endless Hook off : Checkpoint 1

  -Endless Hook on : Checkpoint 2

  -Hammer other tees off : Checkpoint 3


You need to get through the 3 checkpoints before you can finish a round!

If you have problems you can watch ko_matrix.map in the editor.
